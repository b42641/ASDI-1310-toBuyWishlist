about the app

toBuy Wishlist will ultimately be the one place for all your wishlists.

Wherever you are, you can instantly save an item to your wishlist.

You hear a favorite song. A friend recommends a new group. A book review catches your eye.

Whatever the inspiration, toBuy Wishlist will capture and help you order your item.
